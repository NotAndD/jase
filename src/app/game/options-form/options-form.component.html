Game options

This is mandatory if you play from smartphone / tablet. Some things will adjust themself to be played without keyboard and with built-in zoom.

You'll be able to add new paragraphs and later save them.

Audio tracks (low volume ambience) will be played as soon as you start the game.

Actions will trigger sound effects as you explore the story.

Tutorial messages will be displayed when finding something new in the game as you play.

Since you already played the game, only new messages will be shown. If you want all the tutorial to get shown once more, click

The tutorial will not prompt automatically, it will only signal that new messages needs to be seen (and you should definitely check it before choosing a new path, unless you want to play with no tips at all)

QTEs will be enabled. As you explore the story, you may be prompted for actions or required to be fast.

IMPOSSIBLE QTEs will be shown as available paths where available (They add flavour but CAN'T be won!)

QTEs will not require you to use your keyboard, you're just going to need your mouse.
